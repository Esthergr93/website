SQUIRREL at the Junge Uni Aktionstag 2014, November 7th

The Intelligent and Interactive Systems (IIS) group from UIBK will participate in the "Junge Uni Aktionstag 2014" ("Young University Open Day 2014") on November 7th, 2014 together with other research groups from this university. IIS will present the SQUIRREL Robotino robot to pupils from Innsbruck. The initiative "Junge Uni" wants to awake joy of science and research in children and adolescents. Seven to sixteen year olds will have the opportunity to see some small tasks and to interact playfully with the robot.

For more information see http://www.uibk.ac.at/jungeuni

Announcement: Workshop on Affordances 2.0: Visual Perception of Affordances and Functional Visual Primitives for Scene Analysis

ECCV 2014, Zurich, Switzerland; Sept. 7, 2014

The workshop will focus on vision related problems and applications of affordances, the visual primitives grounded in the "functionality" of objects. We extend you a warm invitation to attend and participate at the workshop.

Further details can be found at http://affordances.info/workshops/ECCV.html.
